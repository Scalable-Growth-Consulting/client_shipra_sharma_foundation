Who We Are

Building a Foundation of Hope

The Shipra Sharma Foundation is driven by a singular belief: Education is the most powerful tool for social change.

Based in India, we work tirelessly to bridge the gap for underprivileged girls who are often left behind. By providing resources, mentorship, and safe learning environments, we aren't just teaching subjects; we are building confidence and nurturing the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Pillars of Change

How we are making a tangible difference every day.

Literacy & Education

Providing books, tuition fees, and stationery to ensure no girl drops out of school due to financial constraints.

Skill Workshops

Beyond the classroom, we teach life skills, digital literacy, and vocational training to foster independence.

Mentorship & Support

Connecting young girls with successful female role models to inspire ambitions and guide career paths.
